12. Classification of Persons

share

Persons are of two parts: Puthujjana persons and Arya persons. Puthujjana persons are four. Arya persons are eight. All persons are twelve.

The four Puthujjana persons are: Duggati Ahetuka person, Sugati Ahetuka person, Dvihetuka person, Trihetuka person.

These persons come to classification by the Patisandhi Citta. Those who received Patisandhi with Akusala Vipaka Upekkha Sahagata Santirana Citta are Duggati Ahetukas. Those who received Patisandhi with Kusala Vipaka Upekkha Sahagata Santirana are Sugati Ahetukas. Those who received Patisandhi with the four Maha Vipaka Nana Vippayutta Cittas are Dvihetukas. Those who received Patisandhi with the thirteen Cittas namely four Maha Vipaka Nana Sampayutta and nine Mahaggata Vipaka are Trihetukas. The Asanna person is also an Ahetuka person.

Sovan Margastha person, Sovan Phalastha person, Sakrdagami Margastha person, Sakrdagami Phalastha person, Anagami Margastha person, Anagami Phalastha person, Arhat Margastha person, Arhat Phalastha person; these eight persons are Arya persons.

The Arhat Phalastha person is called Ashaiksha person and the remaining seven Arya persons are called Shaiksha persons.

Cittas obtained by Persons

Kriya Javana and Appana Javana are not obtained for Dvihetukas and Ahetukas. Nana Sampayutta Maha Vipaka are also not obtained for Sugati Ahetuka and Dvihetuka. All eight Nana Sampayutta Vippayutta Maha Vipakas are not obtained for Duggati Ahetuka.

Kusala Akusala Javanas are not obtained for Arhats among Trihetukas. Kriya Javanas are not obtained for Shaikshas and Puthujjanas. Drishtigata Sampayutta Cittas and Vicikiccha Sahagata Cittas are not obtained for Shaikshas. The two Dvesha Mula Cittas are also not obtained for Anagamis.

Lokuttara Javana is obtained only for the respective Margastha Phalastha persons. According to the mentioned facts, the complete number of Cittas obtained for each person should be found.

Excluding the nine Mahaggata Vipakas, the remaining eighty Cittas are Vithi Cittas. Since Mahaggata Vipakas are Dvara Vimukta, they are not Vithi Cittas. The number of Vithi Cittas obtained for each person is as follows. Here, the maximum number of Cittas that can be obtained for each person is shown.

  1. For Duggati Ahetuka - 37
  2. For Sugati Ahetuka - 41
  3. For Dvihetuka - 41
  4. For Trihetuka Puthujjana - 54
  5. For Sovan Margastha - 1
  6. For Sovan Phalastha - 50
  7. For Sakrdagami Margastha - 1
  8. For Sakrdagami Phalastha - 50
  9. For Anagami Margastha - 1
  10. For Anagami Phalastha - 48
  11. For Arhat Margastha - 1
  12. For Arhat Phalastha - 44

Duggati Ahetuka Cittas 37-

Akusala Cittas 12, Ahetuka 17 excluding Hasituppada, Maha Kusala 8.

Sugati Ahetuka Cittas 41-

Akusala 12, Ahetuka 17 excluding Hasituppada, Maha Kusala 8, Nana Vippayutta Maha Vipaka 4. Dvihetuka's Cittas are also these same ones.

Trihetuka Puthujjana's Cittas 54-

Akusala 12, Ahetuka 17 excluding Hasituppada, Maha Kusala 8, Maha Vipaka 8, Mahaggata Kusala 9.

Margastha's Cittas, only the respective Magga Citta.

Sovan Phalastha's Cittas 50-

Lobha Mula Ditthi Vippayutta 4, Dvesha Mula 2, Uddhacca Sahagata Citta, Ahetuka 17 excluding Hasituppada, Maha Kusala 8, Maha Vipaka 8, Mahaggata Kusala 9, Sovan Phala Citta.

Sakrdagami Phalastha's Cittas 50-

From the fifty Cittas of Sovan Phalastha, remove Sovan Phala and include Sakrdagami Phala instead.

Anagami Phalastha's Cittas 48-

Ditthi Vippayutta Cittas 4, Uddhacca Sahagata Citta, Ahetuka 17 excluding Hasituppada, Maha Kusala 8, Maha Vipaka 8, Mahaggata Kusala 9, Anagami Phala Citta.

Arhat Phalastha's Cittas 44-

Ahetuka 18, Maha Vipaka 8, Maha Kriya 8, Mahaggata Kriya 9, Arhat Phala Citta.

When counting Cittas for ordinary beings who have not attained Jhana, the number of Cittas should be taken excluding Mahaggata Cittas.
