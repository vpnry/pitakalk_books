1. 28 Material Phenomena

share

The four Bhuta Rupas: Pathavi Dhatu - Apo Dhatu - Tejo Dhatu - Vayo Dhatu; the five Prasada Rupas: Cakkhu Prasada - Sota Prasada - Ghana Prasada - Jivha Prasada - Kaya Prasada; the five Gocara Rupas: Rupa - Sabda - Gandha - Rasa - Sprastavya; the two Bhava Rupas: Stri Bhava Rupa - Purusa Bhava Rupa; Hadaya Rupa; Jivita Rupa; Ahara Rupa; these are the eighteen Nipphanna Rupas.

Akasa Dhatu; the five Vikara Rupas: Kaya Vinnatti - Vaci Vinnatti - Lahuta - Muduta - Kammannata; the four Laksana Rupas: Upacaya - Santati - Jarata - Aniccata; these are the ten Anipphanna Rupas.

Eighteen Nipphanna Rupas and ten Anipphanna Rupas make twenty-eight Rupas.

(Memorize these twenty-eight Rupas.)

There is no separate Rupa called Sprastavya. What is called Sprastavya in Gocara Rupas refers to the three Bhuta Rupas: Pathavi - Tejo - Vayo. Therefore, when counting the eighteen Nipphanna Rupas, Sprastavya should be omitted. Otherwise, they would be counted as nineteen Nipphanna Rupas.

The main ones among Rupas are the four Bhuta Rupas. The remaining twenty-four Rupas arise dependent on Bhuta Rupas. Therefore, they are called Upadaya Rupas. The group of Rupas is also divided into two as four Bhuta Rupas and twenty-four Upadaya Rupas.
