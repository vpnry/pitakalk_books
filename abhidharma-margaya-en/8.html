7. Seventh Chapter

share

Lesson 1

Since the 170 ultimate realities - 89 consciousnesses, 52 mental factors, 28 material phenomena, and Nibbana - are shown in various groups, this seventh chapter is called the Compendium of Categories (Samuccaya Sangaha).

Compendium of Categories: It consists of four parts: Compendium of Unwholesome Categories, Compendium of Mixed Categories, Compendium of Requisites of Enlightenment, and Compendium of All.
