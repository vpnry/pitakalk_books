10. Summary of Cetasikas in Akusala Cittas

share

You now know which Cetasikas associate with which Cittas. What should be done from now on is to find the number of Cetasikas associating with each Citta. From here onwards, the number of Cetasikas associating with each Citta will be shown in the order Cittas were presented in the Citta Chapter.

There are twenty-seven Cetasikas associating with Akusala Cittas: thirteen Annasamana Cetasikas and fourteen Akusala Cetasikas. To make it easy for you to find Cetasikas, first find the Cetasikas that associate with all Akusala Cittas among those twenty-seven Cetasikas. If you first keep in mind that the fourteen Cetasikas: seven Sabba Citta Sadharana Cetasikas, three Pakinnaka Cetasikas Vitakka - Vicara - Viriya, and four Akusala Sadharana Cetasikas Moha - Ahirika - Anottappa - Uddhacca associate with all Akusala Cittas, you will be able to easily find the other Cetasikas associating with each Citta.

In the first Lobha Mula Citta which is Somanassa Sahagata Ditthigata Sampayutta Asankharika Citta, apart from the fourteen Cetasikas mentioned above, the three Annasamana Cetasikas Adhimokkha - Piti - Chanda and the two Akusala Cetasikas Lobha - Ditthi associate additionally.

1. In the first Lobha Mula Citta, nineteen Cetasikas associate: thirteen Annasamana Cetasikas, four Akusala Sadharana, Lobha, and Ditthi.

2. In the second Lobha Mula Citta, twenty-one Cetasikas associate: the nineteen Cetasikas associating with the first Citta, and the two Thina - Middha. Thina-Middha associate only sometimes.

3. In the third Lobha Mula Citta, nineteen Cetasikas associate: thirteen Annasamana, four Akusala Sadharana, and Lobha - Mana.

4. In the fourth Citta, twenty-one Cetasikas associate: the Cetasikas associating with the third Citta and the two Thina-Middha.

5. In the fifth Citta, eighteen Cetasikas associate: the nineteen Cetasikas associating with the first Citta excluding Piti.

6. In the sixth Citta, twenty Cetasikas associate: the twenty-one Cetasikas associating with the second Citta excluding Piti.

7. In the seventh Citta, eighteen Cetasikas associate: the nineteen Cetasikas associating with the third Citta excluding Piti.

8. In the eighth Citta, twenty Cetasikas associate: the twenty-one Cetasikas associating with the fourth Citta excluding Piti.

9. In the first Dvesha Mula Citta, twenty Cetasikas associate: twelve Annasamana excluding Piti, four Akusala Sadharana, and four Dosa - Issa - Macchariya - Kukkucca. Issa - Macchariya - Kukkucca associate only on some occasions. When they associate, only one of them associates.

10. In the second Dvesha Mula Citta, twenty-two Cetasikas associate: the twenty Cetasikas associating with the first Citta, and Thina - Middha.

11. In the first Moha Mula Citta, fifteen Cetasikas associate: ten Annasamana excluding Adhimokkha - Chanda - Piti, four Akusala Sadharana, and Vicikiccha.

12. In the second Moha Mula Citta, fifteen Cetasikas associate: eleven Annasamana excluding Chanda - Piti, and four Akusala Sadharana.
