19. The One Hundred and Twenty-One Cittas

share

Master these one hundred and twenty-one Cittas well so that they are not forgotten. If this is forgotten, further learning is impossible.

Akusala Cittas

  1. Somanassa-sahagata Ditthigata-sampayutta Asankharika Citta
  2. Somanassa-sahagata Ditthigata-sampayutta Sasankharika Citta
  3. Somanassa-sahagata Ditthigata-vippayutta Asankharika Citta
  4. Somanassa-sahagata Ditthigata-vippayutta Sasankharika Citta
  5. Upekkha-sahagata Ditthigata-sampayutta Asankharika Citta
  6. Upekkha-sahagata Ditthigata-sampayutta Sasankharika Citta
  7. Upekkha-sahagata Ditthigata-vippayutta Asankharika Citta
  8. Upekkha-sahagata Ditthigata-vippayutta Sasankharika Citta

These eight Cittas are Lobha Mula Cittas.

  1. Domanassa-sahagata Patigha-sampayutta Asankharika Citta
  2. Domanassa-sahagata Patigha-sampayutta Sasankharika Citta

These two Cittas are Dosa Mula Cittas.

  1. Upekkha-sahagata Vicikiccha-sampayutta Citta
  2. Upekkha-sahagata Uddhacca-sampayutta Citta

These two Cittas are Moha Mula Cittas.

Eight Lobha Mula Cittas, two Dosa Mula Cittas, and two Moha Mula Cittas make twelve Akusala Cittas.

Ahetuka Cittas

  1. Upekkha-sahagata Cakkhu Vinnana
  2. Upekkha-sahagata Sota Vinnana
  3. Upekkha-sahagata Ghana Vinnana
  4. Upekkha-sahagata Jivha Vinnana
  5. Dukkha-sahagata Kaya Vinnana
  6. Upekkha-sahagata Sampaticchana
  7. Upekkha-sahagata Santirana

These seven are Akusala Vipaka Cittas.

  1. Upekkha-sahagata Cakkhu Vinnana
  2. Upekkha-sahagata Sota Vinnana
  3. Upekkha-sahagata Ghana Vinnana
  4. Upekkha-sahagata Jivha Vinnana
  5. Sukha-sahagata Kaya Vinnana
  6. Upekkha-sahagata Sampaticchana
  7. Somanassa-sahagata Santirana
  8. Upekkha-sahagata Santirana

These eight Cittas are Ahetuka Kusala Vipaka Cittas.

  1. Upekkha-sahagata Pancadvaravajjana
  2. Upekkha-sahagata Manodvaravajjana
  3. Somanassa-sahagata Hasituppada

These three Cittas are Ahetuka Kriya Cittas.

Seven Akusala Vipaka Cittas, eight Kusala Vipaka Cittas, and three Kriya Cittas make eighteen (18) Ahetuka Cittas.

Kamavacara Sobhana Cittas

  1. Somanassa-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Asankharika Citta
  2. Somanassa-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Sasankharika Citta
  3. Somanassa-sahagata Nana-vippayutta Asankharika Citta
  4. Somanassa-sahagata Nana-vippayutta Sasankharika Citta
  5. Upekkha-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Asankharika Citta
  6. Upekkha-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Sasankharika Citta
  7. Upekkha-sahagata Nana-vippayutta Asankharika Citta
  8. Upekkha-sahagata Nana-vippayutta Sasankharika Citta

These eight are Kamavacara Kusala Cittas.

There are also eight Sahetuka Kamavacara Vipaka Cittas, such as Somanassa-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Asankharika Citta etc.

There are also eight Sahetuka Kamavacara Kriya Cittas, such as Somanassa-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Asankharika Citta etc.

Thus, there are twenty-four Kamavacara Sobhana Cittas.

Rupavacara Cittas

  1. Pathamajjhana Kusala Citta accompanied by Vitakka, Vicara, Piti, Sukha, Ekaggata
  2. Dutiyajjhana Kusala Citta accompanied by Vicara, Piti, Sukha, Ekaggata
  3. Tatiyajjhana Kusala Citta accompanied by Piti, Sukha, Ekaggata
  4. Catutthajjhana Kusala Citta accompanied by Sukha, Ekaggata
  5. Pancamajjhana Kusala Citta accompanied by Upekkha, Ekaggata

These five Cittas are Rupavacara Kusala Cittas.

There are also five Rupavacara Vipaka Cittas, such as Pathamajjhana Vipaka Citta accompanied by Vitakka, Vicara, Piti, Sukha, Ekaggata etc.

There are also five Rupavacara Kriya Cittas, such as Pathamajjhana Kriya Citta accompanied by Vitakka, Vicara, Piti, Sukha, Ekaggata etc.

Five Kusala Cittas, five Vipaka Cittas, and five Kriya Cittas make fifteen Rupavacara Cittas.

Arupavacara Cittas

  1. Akasanancayatana Kusala Citta
  2. Vinnanancayatana Kusala Citta
  3. Akincannayatana Kusala Citta
  4. Nevasanna-nasannayatana Kusala Citta

Thus, there are four Arupavacara Kusala Cittas.

Similarly, there are four Arupavacara Vipaka Cittas and four Kriya Cittas. Four Kusala, four Vipaka, and four Kriya make twelve Arupavacara Cittas.

Lokuttara Cittas

  1. Pathamajjhana Sotapatti Magga Citta accompanied by Vitakka, Vicara, Piti, Sukha, Ekaggata
  2. Dutiyajjhana Sotapatti Magga Citta accompanied by Vicara, Piti, Sukha, Ekaggata
  3. Tatiyajjhana Sotapatti Magga Citta accompanied by Piti, Sukha, Ekaggata
  4. Catutthajjhana Sotapatti Magga Citta accompanied by Sukha, Ekaggata
  5. Pancamajjhana Sotapatti Magga Citta accompanied by Upekkha, Ekaggata

These five Cittas are Sotapatti Magga Cittas.

There are also five Sakadagami Magga Cittas, such as Pathamajjhana Sakadagami Magga Citta accompanied by Vitakka, Vicara, Piti, Sukha, Ekaggata etc. Similarly, there are five Anagami Magga Cittas and five Arahat Magga Cittas each.

Thus, there are twenty Lokuttara Kusala Cittas.

There are five Sotapatti Phala Cittas, such as Pathamajjhana Sotapatti Phala Citta accompanied by Vitakka, Vicara, Piti, Sukha, Ekaggata etc., five Sakadagami Phala Cittas, five Anagami Phala Cittas, and five Arahat Phala Cittas, making twenty Lokuttara Vipaka Cittas.

Twenty Kusala and twenty Vipaka make forty Lokuttara Cittas.

Fifty-four Kamavacara Cittas, fifteen Rupavacara Cittas, twelve Arupavacara Cittas, and forty Lokuttara Cittas make one hundred and twenty-one (121) Cittas.

End of the First Chapter.
