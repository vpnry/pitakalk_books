18. Nirodha Samapatti Vithi

share

Na - Da - Ma - Pa - U - A - Go - Jhana - Jhana - Time without consciousness - Pha - Bha.

For the Tikkhapanna, this Vithi arises without the Parikamma Javana.

When the person entering Nirodha Samapatti enters the Nevasannanasannayatana Jhana, the Jhana consciousness arises only twice and ceases, without continuing like the Bhavanga consciousness as in other Samapatti Vithis, due to the power of the preparation made to prevent the arising of consciousness. Entering Jhanas in order from the First Jhana and contemplating their formations is the preparation for stopping the arising of consciousness.

Even if they are attainers of the eight attainments, worldlings and Sotapanna and Sakadagami persons cannot enter Nirodha Samapatti because Kamaraga (Sensual Lust), which is an obstacle to concentration, is not abandoned in them. Entering Nirodha Samapatti exists only in the Kama Loka and Rupa Loka. It does not exist in the Arupa Loka. The absence of Rupavacara attainments there is the reason for the absence of Nirodha Samapatti in the Arupa Loka.

Professor

Most Venerable Rerukane Chandawimala Maha Nayaka Thero
