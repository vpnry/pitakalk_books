Iddhipada

share
  1. Chandiddhipada
  2. Cittiddhipada
  3. Viriyiddhipada
  4. Vimansiddhipada

Thus there are four Iddhipadas.

Iddhi means the accomplishment (success) of something. There are various kinds of Iddhis. Among those Iddhis, in this discussion of Bodhipakkhiya Dhammas, what is taken as Iddhi is the group of Mahaggata Lokuttara Dhammas known as Jhana, Abhinna, Magga, Phala generated by meditation in the Buddha Sasana. What are called Iddhipada are the four Dhammas Chanda, Citta, Viriya, Vimansa which are primarily helpful to reach those Iddhis, to obtain them.

It is very difficult to generate Mahaggata Lokuttara Dhammas generated by Yoga Karma or meditation power in the Buddha Sasana. They cannot be generated by weak Chanda etc. They can be generated only by strong Chanda etc. which cannot be destroyed even by a great danger. Therefore, it should be known that what become Iddhipada are the four Dhammas Chanda etc. which are strong by becoming Adhipati.
