Satipatthana

share
  1. Kayanupassana Satipatthana,
  2. Vedananupassana Satipatthana,
  3. Cittanupassana Satipatthana,
  4. Dhammanupassana Satipatthana

Thus there are four Satipatthanas.

Sati that is well established in objects such as Kaya so that the mind cannot scatter to various objects is called Satipatthana. Although it is one as a Dhamma, it has been preached as four according to the objects such as Kaya where it is established.

Sati maintained in inhalation and exhalation and in body parts such as hair is called Kayanupassana Satipatthana.

Sati maintained in the ninefold Vedanas is called Vedananupassana Satipatthana.

Sati maintained in the Citta which is sixteen-fold such as Saraga etc. is called Cittanupassana Satipatthana.

Sati maintained in Dhammas such as Nivarana which are of five divisions is called Dhammanupassana Satipatthana.
