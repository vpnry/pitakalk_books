12. Vipassana Meditation Subject

share

Lesson 12

The meditation that leads beings to Nibbana by liberating them from the suffering of Samsara, having given the four paths and four fruits of Sotapanna, Sakadagami, Anagami, and Arahant, is Vipassana meditation. The seven purifications, the three characteristics, the three contemplations, the ten Vipassana Nanas, the three Vimokkhas, and the three Vimokkhamukhas are the main facts to be known regarding Vipassana meditation.
