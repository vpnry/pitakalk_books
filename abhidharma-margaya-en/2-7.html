7. How the Eight Lobhamula Cittas Arise

share

(1) The Somanassa-sahagata Ditthigata-sampayutta Asankharika Citta arises when one steals property belonging to others with great joy and without anyone's inducement, holding some wrong view such as "There is no fault in taking what the rich man has in excess" or "There is no fault in taking this wealth earned unjustly"; or when one eats food enjoying the taste with great joy and without anyone's inducement, holding a wrong view such as "These are given to us by God".

(2) The Somanassa-sahagata Ditthigata-sampayutta Sasankharika Citta arises when one does the above actions due to the inducement of another or by making one's own hesitant mind effortless by oneself.

(3) The Somanassa-sahagata Ditthigata-vippayutta Asankharika Citta arises when one steals others' property with joy without holding any wrong view and without anyone's inducement, or when one eats delicious food with joy without holding a wrong view and without anyone's inducement.

(4) The Somanassa-sahagata Ditthigata-vippayutta Sasankharika Citta arises when one does the above actions (in 3) due to the inducement of others or by making one's own mind effortless by oneself.

(5) The Upekkha-sahagata Ditthigata-sampayutta Asankharika Citta arises when one steals others' property without joy (with neutral feeling) and without anyone's inducement, holding some wrong view such as "There is no sin in taking from a man who has excess"; or when one drinks intoxicants without joy and without anyone's inducement, holding a wrong view such as "These are given by God".

(6) The Upekkha-sahagata Ditthigata-sampayutta Sasankharika Citta arises when one does the above actions (in 5) due to the inducement of others or by making one's own hesitant mind effortless by oneself.

(7) The Upekkha-sahagata Ditthigata-vippayutta Asankharika Citta arises when one commits stealing etc. without holding a wrong view, without joy, and without anyone's inducement.

(8) The Upekkha-sahagata Ditthigata-vippayutta Sasankharika Citta arises when one does so due to the inducement of someone or by making one's own hesitant mind effortless by oneself.

Lobhamula Cittas arising as the commission of Akusala Kamma Pathas like stealing are powerful in giving results. Lobhamula Cittas arising as attachment to wife, children, and wealth are weak. Their giving of results is also uncertain. The Ditthigata-sampayutta Akusala Citta is more powerful than the Ditthigata-vippayutta Citta.
