Gantha

share

There are four Ganthas namely Abhijjhakayagantha - Vyapada Kayagantha - Silabbata Paramasa Kayagantha - Idamsaccabhinivesa Kayagantha.

'Abhijjha' Kayagantha is Lobha. All Lobha existing as Kamaraga - Ruparaga - Aruparaga is included in it. 'Vyapada Kayagantha' is Dosa arising in various ways. 'Silabbata Paramasa Kayagantha' is the Micchaditthi which firmly grasps practices like Go-Sila, Go-Vrata etc. which are not causes for release from Samsara suffering, as the path to Nibbana. 'Idamsaccabhinivesa Kayagantha' is the Ditthi which firmly grasps a wrong thing such as "This alone is the truth, everything else is false" and "There is a permanent soul".

When Silabbata Paramasa - Idamsaccabhinivesa are taken by way of Dhammas, both are the single Ditthi Cetasika. Therefore, by way of Dhammas, there are only three Ganthas namely Lobha - Dosa - Ditthi. These three Dhammas connect the present existence and the future existence so that the being cannot get out of Samsara and the succession of existences does not cut off after death. Therefore, those Dhammas are called Gantha.
