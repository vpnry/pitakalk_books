3. Third Chapter

share

Now you know the consciousnesses and mental factors. To complete your knowledge of consciousness and mental factors, you should learn how they arise (thought processes). To be able to learn them, first you should learn the classification of consciousness and mental factors by way of feeling, etc. Therefore, from here onwards, the classification of consciousness is presented by way of feeling, roots, function, doors, objects, and bases.
