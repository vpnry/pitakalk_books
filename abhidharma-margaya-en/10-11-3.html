Mudita Meditation

share

Mudita meditation is the nature of being happy about happy persons. The yogi who wishes to do it to obtain Jhana should take as the object a dear person who has dear and pleasing children, good relatives and friends, beautiful houses, vehicles, clothes, ornaments, food and drinks, who is healthy, and lives with bodily and mental happiness, and practice Mudita meditation saying "This person lives happily without any deficiency, without any bodily or mental suffering. Oh, it is good! Oh, it is good!!". Thus, starting from one person, one should gradually increase Mudita. Considering that because of this person, his wife and children live happily, one should practice Mudita towards them. Considering that because of this person, his relatives, friends, and neighbors live happily, one should practice Mudita towards them. Thus, gradually one should practice Mudita towards one's friends, neutral persons, and enemies. After reaching the state where one can practice Mudita equally towards everyone, one can obtain Mudita Jhana.

Those who wish to practice Mudita in the general method should consider the happiness of beings and practice Mudita saying "These beings live happily. Oh, it is good! Oh, it is good!". There is no being who cannot be considered as happy. In the Brahma world, majestic Brahma kings live very happily for thousands of aeons feeding on joy. In Deva worlds such as Chatummaharajika, many Devas live happily in extremely beautiful mansions shining with gems, surrounded by nymphs, delighting their senses. In the human world, kings, millionaires, and ordinary wealthy people also eat and drink well, dress well, and live happily with their relatives and friends. Poor people also somehow obtain the food and drinks they need and become happy. Beings in Apaya will also one day be free from that suffering and become happy. Thus, considering the happiness of those respective beings with wisdom, one can practice Mudita towards all.
