Upadana

share

There are four Upadanas namely Kamupadana - Ditthupadana - Silabbatupadana - Attavadupadana.

The Dhamma which grasps something firmly so as not to let go easily is called Upadana. The Lobha which firmly grasps sense objects like Rupa etc. is called 'Kamupadana'. The wrong opinion which firmly grasps "This is the truth" is called 'Ditthupadana'. The nature which firmly grasps wrong Silas - wrong Vratas which are not the path to Nibbana, as the path to Nibbana is 'Silabbatupadana'. The nature which firmly grasps Rupa etc. as self is 'Attavadupadana'.

Kamupadana is Lobha Cetasika. The remaining three Upadanas are the single Ditthi Cetasika by way of Dhammas. Therefore, when taken by way of Dhammas, there are two Upadanas namely Lobha - Ditthi.
