Magganga

share

Sammaditthi, Sammasankappa, Sammavaca, Sammakammanta, Sammaajiva, Sammavayama, Sammasati, Samma Samadhi - these eight Dhammas are called Maggangas.

By these eight Dhammas such as Sammaditthi etc. arising associated with a single Citta at once in Lokuttara state, the destruction of Kilesas and the realization of Nibbana are accomplished. Here, by 'Magga' (Path), the meaning of "means" (Upaya) is expressed. It is the means for the destruction of Kilesas and the realization of Nibbana. By 'Anga' (Factor), the meaning of "limb" or "part" is expressed. The parts of the means for destroying Kilesas and realizing Nibbana are called Maggangas. There are four states in this Lokuttara Magga. Those states are respectively called Sotapatti Magga, Sakadagami Magga, Anagami Magga, Arahatta Magga. The eight Maggangas should be understood as stated in the Missaka Sangaha.

These Bodhipakkhiya Dhammas which are thirty-seven by classification, become fourteen as Paramattha Dhammas namely Smrti, Viriya, Chanda, Citta, Panna, Saddha, Ekaggata, Piti, Passaddhi, Tatramajjhattata, Vitakka, Samma Vaca, Samma Kammanta, Samma Ajiva.

Among these fourteen Dhammas, the nine Dhammas Vitakka, Passaddhi, Piti, Tatramajjhattata, Chanda, Citta, and the three Viratis appear in only one place each. Viriya which is one Dhamma appears in nine places as four Sammappadhanas, Viriyiddhipada, Viriyindriya, Viriyabala, Viriya Sambojjhanga, Sammavayama. Sati which is one Dhamma appears in eight places as four Satipatthanas, Satindriya, Satibala, Sati Sambojjhanga, Sammasati. Ekaggata appears in four places as Samadhindriya, Samadhibala, Samadhi Sambojjhanga, Samma Samadhi. Panna Cetasika appears in five places as Vimansiddhipada, Pannindriya, Pannabala, Dhammavicaya Sambojjhanga, Sammaditthi. Saddha Cetasika appears in two places as Saddhindriya, Saddhabala.

All these Bodhipakkhiya Dhammas are generally found in all Lokuttara Cittas. In Lokuttara Cittas such as Dutiya Jhana, Samma Sankappa is not found, and in Lokuttara Cittas of Catuttha Jhana and Pancama Jhana, both Samma Sankappa and Piti Sambojjhanga are not found. When fulfilling Vishuddhis with the expectation of reaching the Lokuttara Magga, Bodhipakkhiya Dhammas are found in Lokiya Cittas as appropriate. All Bodhipakkhiya Dhammas are not found at once in Lokiya Cittas as in Lokuttara Citta.
