13. Summary of Cetasikas in Rupavacara - Arupavacara Cittas

share

In Mahaggata Cittas, thirteen Annasamana Cetasikas and twenty-two Sobhana Cetasikas excluding the three Virati associate. The difference in association of Cetasikas there is according to the Jhanas.

In the three Rupavacara Kusala - Vipaka - Kriya First Jhana Cittas, thirty-five Cetasikas associate: thirteen Annasamana and twenty-two Sobhana Cetasikas excluding Virati. In the three Second Jhana Cittas, thirty-four Cetasikas associate excluding Vitakka from those Cetasikas. In the three Third Jhana Cittas, thirty-three Cetasikas associate excluding both Vitakka - Vicara. In the three Fourth Jhana Cittas, thirty-two Cetasikas associate excluding all three Vitakka - Vicara - Piti. In the three Fifth Jhana Cittas, thirty Cetasikas associate excluding Vitakka - Vicara - Piti - Appamanna.

Since Arupavacara Cittas are also Fifth Jhana Cittas, the same thirty Cetasikas associating with Rupavacara Fifth Jhana Cittas associate with them.

Summary of Cetasikas in Lokuttara Cittas

Thirteen Annasamana and twenty-three Sobhana Cetasikas excluding Appamanna are the Cetasikas associating with Lokuttara Cittas. There too, the difference in association of Cetasikas is according to the Jhanas.

In the eight Lokuttara First Jhana Cittas, thirty-six Cetasikas associate: thirteen Annasamana and twenty-three Sobhana Cetasikas excluding Appamanna. In the eight Second Jhana Cittas, thirty-five Cetasikas associate excluding Vitakka. In the eight Third Jhana Cittas, thirty-four Cetasikas associate excluding both Vitakka and Vicara. In the sixteen Fourth Jhana and Fifth Jhana Cittas, thirty-three Cetasikas associate excluding Vitakka, Vicara, and Piti.

Lokuttara Cittas arise taking Nibbana as the object. Appamanna Cetasikas arise taking beings as the object. Due to the difference in objects, Appamanna does not arise in Lokuttara Citta. Virati does not associate with Mahaggata Cittas because those Cittas arise taking Kasina etc. as objects.

The highest number of Cetasikas associate at once in Lokuttara First Jhana Cittas. Although it is said that thirty-eight Cetasikas associate with Kamavacara Kusala generally, the maximum number of Cetasikas that can associate at one time there is thirty-four. The lowest number of Cetasikas associate in Dvi Panca Vinnana.

Here ends the Second Chapter.
