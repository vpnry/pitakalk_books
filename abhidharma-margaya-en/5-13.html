13. How Cittas are Obtained in Bhumis

share

All these eighty Vithi Cittas are obtained in the Kamavacara Bhumi as appropriate for each person.

In the Rupavacara Bhumi, excluding the sixteen Cittas namely the two Dvesha Mula Cittas, the six Ghana - Jivha - Kaya Vinnanas, and the eight Maha Vipakas, the remaining sixty-four Vithi Cittas are obtained.

In the Arupavacara Bhumi, the forty-two Cittas namely the eight Lobha Mula Cittas, the two Moha Mula Cittas, Manodvaravajjana, the eight Maha Kusalas, the eight Maha Kriyas, the eight Arupavacara Kusala Kriyas, and the seven Lokuttara Cittas excluding the first Path are obtained.

Not even a single Citta is obtained in the Asanna Bhumi.

Counts in brief

  1. In Kama Bhumi - 80
  2. In Rupa Bhumi - 64
  3. In Arupa Bhumi - 42

All Vithi Cittas are obtained in the Kamavacara Bhumi because the six doors and all persons are found there. Certain Cittas are not obtained in Rupa and Arupa Bhumis because the doors where those Cittas arise are not there. Dvesha Mula Cittas are not obtained there because Dvesha, which is a hindrance (Nivarana), is an obstacle to Jhanas. Lobha is also an obstacle to Jhanas. However, not all Lobha becomes a hindrance and obstructs Jhanas. Therefore, Lobha Mula Cittas are obtained in Rupavacara and Arupavacara Bhumis. There is no Dvesha that is not a hindrance. Therefore, Dvesha Mula Cittas are completely not obtained in Rupavacara and Arupavacara Bhumis.

Tadarammana is also a part of Cittas obtained only for Kama beings. They do not exist in Rupa and Arupa worlds. Therefore, Tadarammana is not obtained in Atimahantarammana Vithis and Manodvara Vibhutarammana Vithis arising in those Bhumis.
