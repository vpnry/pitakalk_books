11. Summary of Cetasikas in Ahetuka Cittas

share

In Ahetuka Cittas, only the twelve Annasamana Cetasikas excluding Chanda associate.

In the five Akusala Vipaka Cakkhu Vinnana etc. Cittas, only the seven Sabba Citta Sadharana associate. In Sampaticchana, ten Annasamana Cetasikas associate excluding Chanda - Piti - Viriya. In Santirana, the same ten associate.

In the five Kusala Vipaka Cakkhu Vinnana etc., only the seven Sabba Citta Sadharana associate. In Sampaticchana, ten Annasamana associate excluding Chanda - Piti - Viriya. In Somanassa Sahagata Santirana, eleven Annasamana associate excluding Chanda - Viriya. In Upekkha Sahagata Santirana, ten Annasamana associate excluding Chanda - Piti - Viriya.

In Ahetuka Kriya Pancadvaravajjana Citta, ten Annasamana Cetasikas associate excluding Chanda - Piti - Viriya. In Manodvaravajjana, eleven Annasamana associate excluding Chanda - Piti. In Hasituppada, twelve Annasamana associate excluding Chanda.
