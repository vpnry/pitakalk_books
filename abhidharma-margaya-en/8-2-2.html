Jhananga

share

There are seven Jhanangas namely Vitakka - Vicara - Piti - Ekaggata - Somanassa - Domanassa - Upekkha.

The mind directs to the object, goes towards the object, travels in the object by the power of Jhanangas. Jhanangas are not needed only for Pancavinnanas. Jhanangas are not needed for those Cittas because they arise right there taking the object which has impinged on the Prasada. Jhanangas are found in all Cittas except Dvipancavinnana. Somanassa - Domanassa - Upekkha, all these three take the single Vedana Cetasika. Therefore, by way of Cetasika Dhammas, there are five Jhanangas namely Vitakka - Vicara - Piti - Ekaggata - Vedana. Domanassa is an Akusala Jhananga. Others are common to both Kusala and Akusala sides.
