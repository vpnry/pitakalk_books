Bala

share

There are nine Bala Dhammas namely Saddhabala Viriyabala Satibala Samadhibala Pannabala Hiribala Ottappabala Ahirikabala Anottappabala.

Saddha etc. are called Bala because they crush opposing Dhammas and because they cannot be shaken by opposing Dhammas. Ditthi - Vicikiccha etc. are opposed to Saddha. Since it cannot be shaken by those Dhammas, Saddha is a Bala. Since it cannot be shaken by laziness, Viriya is a Bala. Since it cannot be shaken by Akusala Dhammas, Sati is a Bala. Since it cannot be shaken by Uddhacca, Samadhi is a Bala. Samadhi is Ekaggata. Since it cannot be shaken by Moha, Panna is a Bala. Since it cannot be shaken by Ahirika, Hiri is a Bala. Since it cannot be shaken by Anottappa, Ottappa is a Bala. Since it cannot be shaken by Hiri, Ahirika is a Bala. Since it cannot be shaken by Ottappa, Anottappa is a Bala. Bala Dhammas are not found in Cittas where Viriya Cetasika is not associated.
