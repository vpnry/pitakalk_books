Samyojana

share

There are ten Samyojanas according to the Suttanta method namely Kamaraga Samyojana - Ruparaga Samyojana - Aruparaga Samyojana - Patigha Samyojana - Mana Samyojana - Ditthi Samyojana - Silabbataparamasa Samyojana - Vicikiccha Samyojana - Uddhacca Samyojana - Avijja Samyojana.

There are ten Samyojanas according to the Abhidharma method namely Kamaraga Samyojana - Bhavaraga Samyojana - Patigha Samyojana - Mana Samyojana - Ditthi Samyojana - Silabbataparamasa Samyojana - Vicikiccha Samyojana - Issa Samyojana - Macchariya Samyojana - Avijja Samyojana.

These Kamaraga etc. Dhammas are called Samyojana because they are cords that bind beings in Samsara without letting them escape from Samsara suffering. The two Rupa-Arupa Tanhas indicated by the name Bhava in Asava etc. are shown here as two namely Ruparaga Samyojana - Aruparaga Samyojana. When Kamaraga Samyojana - Ruparaga Samyojana - Aruparaga Samyojana, these three are taken by way of Dhammas, they are the single Lobha Cetasika. Ditthi Samyojana - Silabbataparamasa Samyojana, both take the single Ditthi Cetasika. Therefore, by way of Cetasika Dhammas, there are nine Samyojanas. Lobha - Dosa - Moha - Ditthi - Mana - Vicikiccha - Uddhacca - Issa - Macchariya are the nine Samyojanas.
