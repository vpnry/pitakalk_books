12. Summary of Cetasikas in Kamasobhana Cittas

share

Thirty-eight Cetasikas associate with Kamasobhana Cittas: thirteen Annasamana and twenty-five Sobhana. Among them, Piti does not associate with Upekkha Sahagata Cittas, and Panna Cetasika does not associate with Nana Vippayutta Cittas. Appamanna and Virati do not associate with Vipaka Cittas. Appamanna associate with Kriya Cittas. Virati do not associate. If you keep these few facts in mind, you will be able to easily find the Cetasikas associating with Kamavacara Sobhana Cittas.

In the first and second Kamavacara Kusala Cittas, all thirty-eight Cetasikas associate: thirteen Annasamana and twenty-five Sobhana. It should be remembered that everywhere Virati and Appamanna associate only sometimes. Since the third and fourth Cittas are Nana Vippayutta, thirty-seven Cetasikas associate excluding Panna Cetasika. Since the fifth and sixth Cittas are Upekkha Sahagata, thirty-seven Cetasikas associate: twelve Annasamana excluding Piti, and twenty-five Sobhana Cetasikas. In the seventh and eighth Cittas, thirty-six Cetasikas associate: twelve Annasamana excluding Piti, and twenty-four Sobhana Cetasikas excluding Panna as they are Nana Vippayutta.

In the first and second Vipaka Cittas, thirty-three Cetasikas associate: thirteen Annasamana and twenty Sobhana Cetasikas excluding Virati and Appamanna. Since the third and fourth Cittas are Nana Vippayutta, thirty-two Cetasikas associate excluding Nana from the above thirty-three. In the fifth and sixth Cittas, thirty-two Cetasikas associate: twelve Annasamana excluding Piti, nineteen Sobhana Sadharana, and Panna. Excluding Nana from them, the remaining thirty-one Cetasikas associate with the seventh and eighth Cittas.

In the first two Kriya Cittas, thirty-five Cetasikas associate: thirteen Annasamana, nineteen Sobhana Sadharana, two Appamanna, and Panna. In the third and fourth Cittas, thirty-four Cetasikas associate excluding Nana from those Cetasikas. In the fifth and sixth Cittas, thirty-four Cetasikas associate: twelve Annasamana excluding Piti, nineteen Sobhana Sadharana, two Appamanna, and Panna. Excluding Panna from them, the remaining thirty-three associate with the seventh and eighth Cittas.
