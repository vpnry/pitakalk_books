Bojjhanga

share
  1. Sati Sambojjhanga
  2. Dhammavicaya Sambojjhanga
  3. Viriya Sambojjhanga
  4. Piti Sambojjhanga
  5. Passaddhi Sambojjhanga
  6. Samadhi Sambojjhanga
  7. Upekkha Sambojjhanga

Thus there are seven Bojjhangas.

At the moment the Lokuttara Magga arises, these seven Dhammas such as Smrti, Dhammavicaya etc. which arise associated with it, are called Bodhi because they help to realize the Truth perfectly without any deficiency by dispelling dangers such as the shrinking and shaking nature of the mind. The members (limbs) of that group of Dhammas called Bodhi are called Bojjhangas.

Sati Sambojjhanga is the Smrti Cetasika mentioned by names such as Satipatthana. By meditating, it grows gradually in the continuum of the Yogavacara and at the moment the Lokuttara Magga Citta arises, it arises associated with it helping the realization of Truth.

Dhammavicaya Sambojjhanga is the Panna Cetasika. That Panna is called Dhammavicaya because it investigates well the Nama Rupa Dhammas which are the ground for Vipassana as Anicca etc. That Panna grows gradually in the field of Sankharas which are the ground for Vipassana and becomes the Lokuttara Magga Nana.

Viriya Sambojjhanga is the strong Viriya indicated by the name Sammappadhana. It also grows gradually, dispels the Kilesas that cause retreating and shrinking in the field of Kusala Dhammas, and assists in the realization of Truth at the moment of Lokuttara Magga.

Piti Sambojjhanga is the Piti Cetasika. It also grows by way of meditation on Sankharas which are the ground for Vipassana, dispels the nature of dissatisfaction in Kusala Dhammas, grows gradually by way of meditation and assists in the realization of Truth at the moment of Magga.

Passaddhi Sambojjhanga is the two Passaddhis, Kaya and Citta. In meditating, it grows gradually, dispels the burning and heating nature of the mind, brings it to a calm and cool state, and assists in the realization of Truth at the moment of Lokuttara Magga.

Samadhi Sambojjhanga is the Ekaggata Cetasika. It also grows gradually by meditation, dispelling the restlessness of the mind, and assists in the realization of Truth.

Upekkha Sambojjhanga is the Tatramajjhattata Cetasika. It also grows gradually by meditation, completely dispelling the shrinking and shaking nature of the mind, and assists in the realization of Truth at the moment of Magga.
