Indriya

share

There are five Indriyas namely Saddhindriya, Viriyindriya, Satindriya, Samadhindriya, Pannindriya. These five Indriyas were also mentioned in the twenty-two Indriyas stated in the Missaka Sangaha. Saddha is twofold as ordinary Saddha and Saddha developed by meditation. In the subject of Bodhipakkhiya Dhammas, what is taken as Saddhindriya is the Saddha developed by meditation. Similarly, what are taken as Viriyindriya etc. are also Viriyindriya etc. developed by meditation.
