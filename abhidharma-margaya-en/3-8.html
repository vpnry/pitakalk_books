8. Association of Akusala Cetasikas

share

Among the Akusala Cetasikas, the four Cetasikas Moha, Ahirika, Anottappa, Uddhacca are Akusala Sadharana (Universal to Akusala). Those four associate with all Akusala Cittas without distinction.

Lobha associates with the eight Lobha Mula Cittas. Ditthi associates with the four Ditthi Sampayutta Cittas. Mana sometimes associates with the four Ditthi Vippayutta Cittas.

The four Dosa, Issa, Macchariya, Kukkucca associate with the two Dvesha Mula Cittas. Among them, the three Cetasikas Issa, Macchariya, Kukkucca associate only on some occasions.

The two Thina, Middha associate with the five Sasankharika Cittas. They also associate only on some occasions. "Vicikiccha" associates only with the Vicikiccha Sahagata Citta.

There are four Cetasikas that associate with all Akusala Cittas. There are three Cetasikas that associate only with Lobha Mula Cittas. There are four Cetasikas that associate only with Dvesha Mula Cittas. There are two Cetasikas that associate only with Akusala Sasankharika Cittas. There is one Cetasika that associates only with Vicikiccha Sahagata Citta. Find the Akusala Cetasikas associating with the twelve Akusala Cittas in order.

Special Facts

If there is wisdom seeing the fault of Akusalas, Akusala Cittas do not arise. Similarly, when there is Hiri loathing Akusala and Ottappa fearing it, Akusala Cittas do not arise. Also when the mind is established in Kusala Dhamma, Akusala Cittas do not arise. For every Akusala Citta to arise, there must be Moha covering its danger, Ahirika not being ashamed of it, Anottappa not fearing it, and Uddhacca shaking. Therefore, it is said that the four Moha, Ahirika, Anottappa, Uddhacca associate with all Akusala Cittas.

The view taking "Five Aggregates are a Soul, are Eternal" etc. arises, and the Mana taking "I am high" etc. arises, only with Lobha which is the attachment to Five Aggregates. Therefore, the two Cetasikas Ditthi and Mana do not arise in any other Citta except Lobha Mula Cittas. Since taking as eternal etc. and taking as high etc. are different from each other, they never arise together in one Citta at the same time. That is why Mana does not associate with Ditthi Sampayutta Cittas.

Macchariya arises due to Lobha clinging to one's own possessions. However, since it arises as not tolerating one's property being common to others, it arises with Dvesha and not with Lobha. That is why it is said that Macchariya arises only in Dvesha Mula Citta.

The two Cetasikas Thina and Middha which weaken the mind and make it unfit for work associate only with the weak Sasankharika Cittas. They associate with Sasankharika Cittas only on some occasions. Not always.
