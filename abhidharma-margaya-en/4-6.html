6. Summary of Bases

share

In this Dhamma, the name “Vatthu” (Base) is used for the places located in the bodies of beings where Cittas arise.

There are six Vatthus or places where Cittas arise: Cakkhu Vatthu, Sota Vatthu, Ghana Vatthu, Jivha Vatthu, Kaya Vatthu, Hadaya Vatthu.

Cakkhu Vatthu is the Cakkhu Pasada mentioned as Cakkhu Dvara in the Summary of Doors. Sota - Ghana - Jivha - Kaya Vatthus are those respective Pasada Rupas themselves.

The special thing among Vatthus is the Hadaya Vatthu. It is a certain subtle element existing depending on about half a cup of blood located in the heart chamber. It is a very subtle thing not seen by the eye - not caught by the hand. It is not present in the heart chamber of the dead. It is also not something caught by medical science. The name Vatthu Rupa is also used for it. That Vatthu Rupa, which helps the arising of Cittas flowing out as if from the heart chamber, does not go out mixed with the blood flowing out of the heart chamber. Most of the Cittas arise in the Hadaya Vatthu.

Beings with complete faculties in the Kama Loka have all six Vatthus. Brahmas in the Rupa Loka do not have Ghana - Jivha - Kaya Vatthus. Arupa beings do not have even a single Vatthu.

By saying that there are no Ghana - Jivha - Kaya Vatthus in Rupa Loka, it should not be taken that Rupavacara Brahmas do not have nose - tongue and body. What they do not have are the Ghana - Jivha - Kaya Pasadas which are the support for Cittas. Cakkhu Vatthu and Sota Vatthu are useful for things helpful for purity such as seeing the Buddha - listening to Dhamma. The three Vatthus Ghana etc. are useful only for the enjoyment of sensual pleasures and nothing else. To Brahmas born by the power of Kama Viraga Bhavana Kusala, the three Vatthus Ghana etc. do not arise. The two Vatthus Cakkhu - Sota arise for the purpose of seeing the Buddha - listening to Dhamma etc.

There are three parts of Cittas: Cittas arising depending on a Vatthu, Cittas arising depending on and not depending on a Vatthu, and Cittas arising without depending on a Vatthu.

The two Cakkhu Vinnanas arise depending on the Cakkhu Vatthu itself. Sota - Ghana - Jivha - Kaya Vinnanas also arise depending on those Vatthus.

The thirty-three Cittas: Pancadvaravajjana, two Sampaticchanas, three Santiranas, eight Mahavipakas, two Dosa Mula Cittas, Sotapatti Magga Citta, Hasituppada, and fifteen Rupavacara Cittas arise depending on the Hadaya Vatthu itself.

The forty-two Cittas: eight Kamavacara Kusala Cittas, four Arupa Kusala Cittas, eight Lobha Mula Cittas, two Moha Mula Cittas, Manodvaravajjana, eight Maha Kriya Cittas, four Arupa Kriya Cittas, and seven Lokuttara Cittas excluding the First Magga arise depending on the Hadaya Vatthu Rupa when arising in Kama - Rupa worlds, and arise without depending on a Vatthu when arising in Arupa world.

The four Arupa Vipaka Cittas arise without depending on a Vatthu.

Part II

There are forty-three Cittas arising depending on a Vatthu. There are forty-two Cittas arising depending on and not depending on a Vatthu. There are four Cittas arising without depending on a Vatthu.

Forty-three Cittas arise depending on a Vatthu because those Cittas do not arise in the Arupa world.

Among those Cittas, Dvi Panca Vinnana - Pancadvaravajjana - Sampaticchana - Santirana - Maha Vipakas do not arise in the Arupa world because the doors where they arise and the functions performed by those Cittas are not there.

Dosa Mula Cittas do not arise there because those Cittas do not arise in Jhana attainers.

Hasituppada Citta is not there because there is no laughter of Arupa beings.

Sotapatti Magga Citta does not arise there because Arupa beings without ears cannot hear Dhamma from others. Except for Lord Buddhas and Pacceka Buddhas, others cannot generate Sotapatti Magga by their own power without hearing Dhamma from others. Buddhas - Pacceka Buddhas do not arise in the Arupa world. There is no way for Arupa beings to hear anything from others. One who has become Sotapanna can attain the remaining Magga Phalas without the help of others. Sometimes those who became Sotapanna in Kama - Rupa worlds are born in the Arupa world. They attain Sakadagami - Anagami - Arahat Magga Phalas there itself and attain Parinibbana.
