3. Bodhipakkhiya Sangaha

share

6th Lesson

Bodhi is a name for the Lokuttara Magga Nana. Dhammas that are helpful for Lokuttara Magga Nana are called Bodhipakkhiya. There are seven groups of Bodhipakkhiya Dhammas namely 'Satipatthana - Sammappadhana - Iddhipada - Indriya - Bala - Bojjhanga - Magganga'. Since there are four Satipatthanas, four Sammappadhanas, four Iddhipadas, five Indriyas, five Balas, seven Bojjhangas, and eight Maggangas, there are thirty-seven Bodhipakkhiya Dhammas individually.
