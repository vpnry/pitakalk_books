13. The Eight Sahetuka Kamavacara Vipaka Cittas

share

Just like Kamavacara Kusala, there are eight Sahetuka Kamavacara Vipaka Cittas (Resultant Consciousness with Roots) according to Feeling, Knowledge, and Prompting.

  1. Somanassa-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Asankharika Citta (Unprompted Consciousness associated with Joy and Knowledge).
  2. Somanassa-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Sasankharika Citta (Prompted Consciousness associated with Joy and Knowledge).
  3. Somanassa-sahagata Nana-vippayutta Asankharika Citta (Unprompted Consciousness associated with Joy and dissociated from Knowledge).
  4. Somanassa-sahagata Nana-vippayutta Sasankharika Citta (Prompted Consciousness associated with Joy and dissociated from Knowledge).
  5. Upekkha-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Asankharika Citta (Unprompted Consciousness associated with Neutral Feeling and Knowledge).
  6. Upekkha-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Sasankharika Citta (Prompted Consciousness associated with Neutral Feeling and Knowledge).
  7. Upekkha-sahagata Nana-vippayutta Asankharika Citta (Unprompted Consciousness associated with Neutral Feeling and dissociated from Knowledge).
  8. Upekkha-sahagata Nana-vippayutta Sasankharika Citta (Prompted Consciousness associated with Neutral Feeling and dissociated from Knowledge).

These are the eight Sahetuka Kamavacara Vipaka Cittas.

These eight Cittas are not the only Cittas that arise as results of Kamavacara Kusala. The eight Kusala Vipaka Cittas mentioned in the Ahetuka Cittas section above are also results of this same Kamavacara Kusala. The results of Kamavacara Kusala are of two types: Ahetuka (Rootless) and Sahetuka (With Roots). These eight Cittas are Sahetuka Kamavacara Vipaka Cittas. The eight Kusala Vipaka Cittas shown in Ahetuka Cittas are Ahetuka Kusala Vipakas.

After the person who performed Kamavacara Kusala dies, these Sahetuka Kamavacara Vipaka Cittas arise in a certain existence as the result of Kamavacara Kusala, belonging to the same mental continuum that existed while living. If the Kusala was very weak, the Kusala Vipaka Upekkha-sahagata Santirana Citta arises first in the new existence as the result of the weak Kusala. The arising of that first Citta in some place after death is itself the being's arrival at that existence. After birth, Ahetuka Kusala Vipaka Cittas such as Eye-consciousness arise as results of Kamavacara Kusala, seeing good forms, hearing good sounds, etc.

The Eight Sahetuka Kamavacara Kriya Cittas

Arahants, who have completely eradicated craving and will not be born in Samsara again in the future, have no merit or demerit. However, Arahants also perform good deeds agreed upon in the world as "merit", such as honoring those worthy of honor, preaching Dhamma, and listening to Dhamma. Although the Cittas arising in Arahants by way of performing those deeds are similar to Kamavacara Kusala Cittas, they do not have the power to produce future results like Kusala. Therefore, they are not Kusala. Since the Cittas arising in the continuum of Arahants only perform those respective functions, they are called "Kriya Cittas" (Functional Consciousness). Sahetuka Kamavacara Kriya Cittas are also eight, just like Kamavacara Kusala.

  1. Somanassa-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Asankharika Citta
  2. Somanassa-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Sasankharika Citta
  3. Somanassa-sahagata Nana-vippayutta Asankharika Citta
  4. Somanassa-sahagata Nana-vippayutta Sasankharika Citta
  5. Upekkha-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Asankharika Citta
  6. Upekkha-sahagata Nana-sampayutta Sasankharika Citta
  7. Upekkha-sahagata Nana-vippayutta Asankharika Citta
  8. Upekkha-sahagata Nana-vippayutta Sasankharika Citta

These eight are the Sahetuka Kamavacara Kriya Cittas.

The eight Kamavacara Kusala Cittas, the eight Sahetuka Kamavacara Vipaka Cittas, and the eight Sahetuka Kamavacara Kriya Cittas make up twenty-four (24) Kamavacara Sobhana Cittas (Beautiful Kamavacara Consciousness). Among them, there are twelve Somanassa-sahagata Cittas. There are twelve Upekkha-sahagata Cittas. There are twelve Nana-sampayutta Cittas. There are twelve Nana-vippayutta Cittas. There are twelve Asankharika Cittas. There are twelve Sasankharika Cittas.

The twelve Akusala Cittas, the eighteen Ahetuka Cittas, and the twenty-four Kamavacara Sobhana Cittas make up fifty-four (54) Kamavacara Cittas.

In the fifty-four Kamavacara Cittas, there are twelve Akusala. There are eight Kusala. There are twenty-three Vipaka. There are eleven Kriya.

  1. Somanassa-sahagata Cittas - 18
  2. Upekkha-sahagata Cittas - 32
  3. Domanassa-sahagata Cittas - 2
  4. Sukha-sahagata Cittas - 1
  5. Dukkha-sahagata Cittas - 1
  6. Asankharika Cittas - 37
  7. Sasankharika Cittas - 17

Remember these numbers of Cittas. If not remembered, it may be troublesome later.
