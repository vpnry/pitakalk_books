There are also five Bala Dhammas namely Saddhabala, Viriyabala, Satibala, Samadhibala, Pannabala. These were also mentioned in the Balas stated in the Missaka Sangaha. The only difference in these is that they are Saddha etc. developed by meditation to the state where they can be helpful for the Lokuttara Magga.
